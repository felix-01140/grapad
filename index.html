Better Live For Others

GRAPAD is a non-profit association that brings together women, men, and young people under an indefinite mandate to enlighten and guide public opinion, aiming to promote peace and sustainable development in the Democratic Republic of Congo, Rwanda, Burundi, and Uganda. It intervenes in the following areas: 1. Protection of children, 2. Education and social communication, 3. Promotion of peace, human rights, and democracy, 4. Environmental protection, and 5. Fight against sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV).

Learn More

Our Vision

The vision of the organization is to promote the social, cultural, economic, and natural potential of Africans in an environment of peace, free from war, discrimination, and human rights violations.

  • Range of action

    The scope of action extends across the entire territory of the Democratic Republic of Congo, Rwanda, Burundi, and Uganda.

  • The overall objective

    GRAPAD aims to enlighten and guide public opinion to promote peace and development in the Democratic Republic of Congo and its neighboring countries, particularly Rwanda, Burundi, and Uganda.

  • Values

    The GRAPAD organization is characterized by the following values: Transparency, Teamwork and compassion, Equality.
